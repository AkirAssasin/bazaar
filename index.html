D'BAZAAR

Participate in D' Bazaar 2017 - be it entrepreneur, player, hero or artist, there is value for you here.

The Bazaar's aim is to provide both young or seasoned entrepreneurs an environment to experiment and learn about starting a business in a real world setting.

That's not all - 50% of the profits will be given to the Agathian Shelter, a shelter that provides a home and support for orphans and children from broken families.

What are you waiting for? It's your chance to shine and go wild!
